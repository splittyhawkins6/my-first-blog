Album of the Week

I Decided News

I Decided by Big Sean, is one of the largest releases so far in 2017. Big Sean, an artist attempting to compete with leaders in the rap industry, combines hypnotic beats with flowing lyrics to prove his worth to the game. The album considers Sean's meaning in life and his thankfulness for his family, specifically his mother.
